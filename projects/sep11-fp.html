Our Goal

as of 2026, there has been a rise of book bans in the united states. Hermit is a tool meant to locate those books to find where they are banned in, and where you can access said books. Hermit is named after the hermit tarot card, which represents inner wisdom and soul searching, which resonates with the goal of hermit.
